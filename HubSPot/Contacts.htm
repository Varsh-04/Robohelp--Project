Contacts

You can create and maintain information, track it, manage the lifecycle stages of the contacts and link contacts with deals and tickets in HubSpot.

 

Create contact

 

You can create a contact in HubSpot and update the progress of contacts with your business. The progress is called Lifecycle stages.

 

Lifecycle stages:

 

  • Subscriber - The person who subscribes or joined any activity in your business.

  • lead - The person who showed interest in your business.

  • Marketing qualified lead - The person who is always active with your business, that is always opens your e-mails, visits your websites repeatedly.

  • Sales qualified lead - The person who is ready to make a purchase.

  • Opportunity - You created a deal with that person.

  • Customer - The person who purchased.

  • Evangelist - The person who always promotes your business, that is who recommends to others.

  • Other - The person who doesn't match the above stages.

All contacts

 

You can view list of all the contacts you added in HubSpot.

 

figure 4

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button, and then press the All contacts tab

A list of all the contacts will display

 

Contact owner

 

You can filter contacts by contact owners's name.

A contact owner is a person who will handle all the activities related to that contact

 

figure 5

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Press Contact owner drop-down

  • Select contact owner's name, and then press the Apply button

A list of all the contacts related to the selected contact owner will display

 

Filter contacts by date

 

You can filter contacts by the date, they were created.

 

figure 6

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Press Create date drop-down, and then select date

A list of all contact created on the selected date will display

 

Last activity date

 

You can filter contacts by the date they were lastly interacted with.

 

figure 7

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Press Last activity date drop-down, and then select date

A list of contact related activity on the selected date will display

 

Lead Status

 

You can view the progress of the contacts with your business.

 

figure 8

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Press Lead status drop-down,

  • Select stage, and then press the Apply button

A list of contacts for the selected stage will display

 

Add views

 

In Add views, it includes All contacts, Marketing contacts, My contacts, My recently assigned contacts, My uncontacted, Needs email follow-up, Needs next action, Unassigned contacts. You can choose what you need to view.

 

figure 9

 

  • Press the Menu button in home screen

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Press the Add views button

  • Choose views, and then press the Apply button

A list of contacts for the selected view will display
