Getting Started

You must first Install the HubSpot app in your mobile device from Play Store.

 

  • Open Play Store app

 

         figure 1

 

  • Type HubSpot in search bar, press the search button

  •  

     

  •                     figure 2

 

  • Press HubSpot icon

  • Press the Install button

          Wait few seconds to complete installation.

 

Sign up

 

To create a new user account, you need to sign up.

 

  • Open HubSpot app

  •  

  •                 figure 3

 

  • Press the Sign up button

  •  

                    figure 4

 

  • Enter details, and press the Next button

 

                                           figure 5

 

  • Open confirmation mail

You will receive an e-mail to the submitted e-mail address

  • Press the Confirm email address button

 

                               figure 6

 

  • Enter password in Password box

The password should satisfy all listed requirements

  • Press the Next button  

 

                              figure 7

 

  • Enter details, and press the Finish sign up button

  • Note: In Website URL box, paste your company website link. if you don't have a website, enter www.yourcompanyname.com

  •  

                          figure 8

 

  • Press the required button

  • Choose according to your company's field

 

You have successfully completed the initial setup. The application is now configured and ready to use.
