HubSpot

 

Company

You can create and keep a record of the company and all the related activities in HubSpot.

 

Create company

 

You can create a profile of the company which is in contact with your business.

 

                       figure 1

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

 

figure 2

 

  • Press the Add Company button

 

figure 3

 

  • Enter the required details, and press the Save button

A company profile will be created and saved in the Companies tab

 

All companies

 

You can view list of all the companies which is tied up with your business activities will display.

 

  figure 4

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

  • Press the All companies tab

A list of all companies will display

 

Company owner

 

You can filter companies by company owner.

A company owner is a person who will handle all the company related activities.


figure 5

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

  • Press Company owner drop-down

  • Select company owner

A list of companies related to the selected company owner will display

 

Filter companies by date

 

You can filter companies by the date when the company profile have been created.

 

   figure 6

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

  • Press Create date drop-down

  • Select date

A list of companies that are created on the selected date will display

 

Last activity date

 

You can view any recently happened updates or interaction with the company in last activity tab.

 

figure 7

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

  • Press Last activity date drop-down

  • Select date

A list of company related activities on the selected date will display

 

Lead Status

 

You can filter and view the current progress of the company, lead status includes stages to track the company progress.

 

Stages:

  • New - Recently created the company profile

  • Open - Just start working on the activities related to that company

  • In Progress - Activities related to that company is going on

  • Open Deal - That company started a deal with your business

  • Unqualified - That company stopped interacting with your business

  • Attempted to Contact - You tried to contact with that company

  • Connected - That company successfully connected with your business

  • Bad Timing - That company will contact you later, not now

 

figure 8

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Companies button

  • Press Lead Status drop-down

  • Select stages, and then press the Apply button

A list of companies for the selected stage will display
