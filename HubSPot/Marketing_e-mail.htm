Marketing Email (Mktg. Email)

Marketing e-mails are sent to multiple customers,leads and prospects at once to inform them about your deals, updates, or announcements. you can view and maintain the marketing e-mail in HubSpot. In HubSpot you can create 8 different types of e-mails. They are,

 

Types:

  • Automated - HubSpot will send the Mktg. e-mails to many customers at once automatically at a scheduled time.

  • Automated A/B - It is same as automated but, you can send the two versions of same e-mail A and B to test the e-mail performance.

  • Blog - HubSpot will automatically send the e-mail, when the subscribers send a blog post

  • Follow-up - HubSpot will automatically send the follow-up e-mail, when a customer fills any forms which is sent by you

  • RSS - When you post any new blogs or updates in your website, HubSpot will create the informations as e-mail will automatically send the e-mail to your subscribers.

  • Regular -  A normal marketing e-mail, you need to send to customers manually.

  • Regular A/B - It is same as regular, but you can send the two versions of same e-mail A and B to test the e-mail performance.

  • Time zone - For international customers, HubSpot will send e-mail according to their time zone. you need to schedule time first.

 

Note: In mobile, you can only send e-mails to your team members. To send marketing e-mails to customers, you need to login to HubSpot on desktop.
