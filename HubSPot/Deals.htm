Deals

You can create deals, assign deals to people, associate deals with company, manage sales pipeline and track deals activities in HubSpot. You can create a even if a customer is interested in a purchase.

 

Create deals

 

Create deals to check the progress of the sales and create deals on future dates.

 

  figure 1

 

  • Press the Deals button in home page

  •  

    figure 2

     

  • Press the Add Deal button

  •  

    figure 3

     

  • Enter the required details

  •  

    figure 4

     

  • Press the add  button in Contacts box, and select contacts

  • Press the Done button

  • Press the add button in Company box, and select company

  • Press the Done button, and then, press the Save button

Make sure all the entered details are correct before creating deal

 

All deals

 

List of all the deals that are created and are not yet closed deals will display.

 

  • Press the Deals button in home screen

Deals will display

 

Deal owner

 

You can filter deals by deal owner and check any unassigned deals are left.

A deal owner is a person who will handle the deals

 

figure 5

 

  • Press the Deals button

  • Press Deal owner drop-down

  • Select deal owner name

  • Press the Apply button

All the deals related to selected deal owner will display

 

Filter deals by date

 

You can filter deals by the date they were created and view the deals that are created in future dates. You can filter deals by days, weeks, months, quarters, years, and last days.

 

figure 6

 

  • Press the Deals button

  • Press Create date drop-down

  • Select the required button

All the deals created on the selected date will display

 

Last activity date

 

You can view activities related to deals that happened recently.

 

figure 7

 

  • Press the Deals button

  • Press Last activity date drop-down

  • Select the required button

All the activity related to deals on selected date will display

 

Close date

 

You can view deals that are closed and the deals that you expect to close on future dates.

 

figure 8

 

  • Press the Deals button

  • Press Close date drop-down

  • Select the required button

  • All the activity related to deals on selected date will display

 

Pipeline

 

A pipeline is a set of stages that goes through a deal from start to end until the deal is closed In all pipeline it displays any other pipelines created by other members expect sales pipeline will display. In sales pipeline it displays in what stages the deals are going on.

 

figure 9

 

  • Press the Deals button

  • Press All pipelines drop-down

  • Select the required option

A list of all deals related to selected pipeline will display
