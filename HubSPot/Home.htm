Home

In Home page, you can view your task for the upcoming week, marketing reports, create notes, tickets and deals.

 

Task

 

Task is a to-do list, you can create a task list with due dates, assign task to people, set priority levels like high, medium and low to task and set reminders.

Associate task

 

You can associate task with people, companies, deals, and  tickets, associated people or companies can view the task in their device.

Associating task is like linking task with people or companies, they can just view and cannot edit task

 

      figure 6

 

  • Press the Add button

  • Select fields to associate task

  • Select options

  • Press the Done button, and then press the Apply button

figure 11

Marketing reports

 

Marketing reports is a e-mail performance dashboard

 

    figure 12

 

In Email sent, opened, and click totals by email send date dashboard, it displays last 30 days e-mail performance.

 

Last 30-days performance:

 

  • SENT - Total number of  e-mails delivered to people

  • OPENED - Total number of e-mails opened by people

  • CLICKS - Total number of  people at least clicked one link inside e-mail.

 

figure 13

 

In Marketing email sent totals with engagement rates dashboard, it displays engagement rates  in percentages

 

Engagement rates:

 

  • E-mail sent - Total number of e-mails sent in last 30 days

  • Open rate - Percentage of people who opened e-mail

  • Click-Through-Rate (CTR) - Percentage of people who at least clicked one link inside e-mail

  • Bounce rate - Percentage of e-mails that failed to reach people's inbox

 

figure 14

 

In New visitor session totals by day with source breakdown dashboard, it displays total number of  people visited your business website first time and by which source that brought people to your site like social media or ads etc..

 

  figure 15

 

In Form submission totals, it displays total number of people submitted your forms in the last 30 days
