HubSpot

 

Feed

HubSpot's Feed section displays recent activity and updates.

 

 figure 1

 

  • Press the Feed button in home screen

Recent activity and updates will display in All activity tab

 

Opened e-mails

 

You can view list of people who opened your e-mails with their name, number of times they opened and last opened time.

E.g., X opened "Email name" 3 times - opened 30 mins ago

 

 

figure 2

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Press the Email opens tab

A list of all opened e-mails will display

 

Click-through e-mails

 

You can view list of people who clicked at least one link inside your e-mail with their name, number of times they clicked the link and when they clicked the link.

E.g., X clicked "link name" twice or repeatedly - clicked 10 mins ago

 

  figure 3

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Press the Email Clicks tab

A list of all clicked-through-e-mails will display

 

Replied e-mails

 

You can view list of people who replied to your email with their names and when they replied.

E.g., X replied to your e-mail - replied 30 mins ago

 

           figure 4

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Email Replies tab

A list of all e-mail replies will display

 

Sent e-mails

 

You can view list of all e-mails you sent to people.

 

figure 5

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Sent Emails tab

A list of all sent e-mails will display

 

Lead Visits

 

You can view list of people who visited your website with their names, what page they opened, when they opened and how many times they visited your site.

E.g., X visited "Page name" 2 times - 20 mins ago

 

figure 6

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Lead Visits tab

A list of all leads who visited your site will  display

 

Prospect Visits

 

You can view list of companies that visited your site  with company name, what page they visited, how many they visited and when they visited.

E.g., X company visited "Page name" once - 10 mins ago

 

figure 7

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Prospect Visits tab

A list of companies will display

 

Document Views

 

You can view list of people who viewed the document that you have shared with them with their names, document name, how many times viewed, when viewed the document.

E.g., X viewed "Document name" 2 times - 10 mins ago

 

figure 8

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Document View tab

A list of people names will display

 

Meetings

 

You can view list of all the activity related to meeting links and scheduled meetings with a customer.

 

figure 9

 

  • Press the Feed button

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Meetings tab

A list of all activity related to meetings will display

 

Forms

 

You can view list of all the activity related to HubSpot forms, or any other forms on your website.

 

figure 10

 

  • Press the Feed button in home page

  • Slide the tabs

  • Press the Forms tab

A list of activity related to forms will display
