HubSpot

 

Tickets

You can create, set priority level, link tickets to deals and contacts in HubSpot.

 

Create ticket

 

You can create a ticket in HubSpot.

 

figure 1

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

 

figure 2

 

  • Press the Add ticket button

 

figure 3

 

  • Enter the required details, and press the Save button

A ticket will be created and saved in the Tickets tab

 

All tickets

 

You can view list of all the tickets which is raised, still in progress, and completed.

 

   figure 4

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

  • Press the All tickets tab

A list of all the tickets will display

 

Ticket owner

 

You can filter the tickets by ticket owner's name.

A ticket owner is a person who will handle the ticket.

 

figure 5

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

  • Press Ticket owner drop-down

  • Select ticket owners name, and then press the Apply button

A list of all the tickets related to the selected ticket owner will display

 

Filter tickets by date

 

You can filter tickets by the date, they were created.

 

figure 6

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

  • Press Create date drop-down, and then select date

A list of all tickets created on the selected date will display

 

Priority

 

You can choose important level of the ticket.

 

figure 7

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

  • Press Priority drop-down, select priority level, and then press the Apply button

A list of all tickets related to the selected priority levels will display

 

Views

 

You can view all tickets, your assigned tickets and unassigned tickets in Add views tab.

 

figure 8

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Tickets button

  • Press the Add views tab

  • Select views, and then press the Apply button

A list of tickets for the selected view will display
