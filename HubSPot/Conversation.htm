HubSpot

 

Conversation

You can view list of all messages related to customer communications in Conversations tab

figure 1

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

List of all received messages will display

 

Create messages

 

You can create and send messages to customers related to your business.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Contacts button

  • Select Contacts to message

 

figure 2

 

  • Press the SMS button

 

figure 3

 

  • Enter the required details, and press the Save button

HubSpot will automatically send messages to customers at scheduled time

 

Unassigned

 

You can view list of messages that have not been assigned to any members.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

figure 4

 

  • Press the Unassigned tab

List of unassigned messages will display

 

Status

 

You can view progress of the message as open or closed. An open message needs a response, while a closed message shows that the conversation has ended.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

figure 5

 

  • Press Status drop-down, and then select status

List of messages will display with their status

 

Channel

 

You can view the messages that are received from different resources.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

figure 6

 

  • Press Channel drop-down

  • Select resources, and then press the Done button

List of messages will display with their resources

 

Contact

 

You can view messages that are received from a particular person.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

  figure 7

 

  • Press Contacts drop-down

  • Select contacts, and then press the Done button

List of messages received from the selected contact will display

 

Assignee

 

You can view messages that are assigned to other team members.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

figure 8

 

  • Press Assignee drop-down

  • Select contacts, and then press the Done button

List of messages assigned for the selected contact will display

 

Date

 

You can view messages that are sent and received on a particular day.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

figure 9

 

  • Press Date drop-down

 

figure 10

 

  • Press the Start date tab

  • Select month, day and year, and press the Done button

  • Press the End date tab

  • Select month, day and year, and then press the Done button

List of messages for the selected date will display

 

All views

 

You can view Assigned to me, All open, All close, Sent messages, Spam, Trash and calls.

 

  • Press the Menu button

  • Press the Conversations button

 

  figure 11

 

  • Press the All views button

 

figure 12

 

  • Select the required fields

A list of all messages related to the selection will display

 

Dashboard

 

In dashboard, you can create your business performance in HubSpot using charts, tables, and numbers.

Note: Dashboards can only be created on desktop. On mobile, you can only view performance.

 

  • Press the Menu button

 

  figure 13

 

  • Press the Dashboards button

Dashboard created on desktop will display
